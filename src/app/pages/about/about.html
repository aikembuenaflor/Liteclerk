Our Roots

Our Background

Liteclerk Corporation is a 100% Filipino-owned software company. We specialize in developing software for POS (Point-Of-Sale), inventory management, and financial accounting.

Starting in 2008 with a small office in Cebu, we have expanded our reach nationwide. Our journey is defined by constant adaptation to the changing technological landscape, from desktop applications to full cloud-native solutions.
